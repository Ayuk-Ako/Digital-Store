Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Digital Store

IPTV (Internet Protocol Television) delivers television content over the internet instead of traditional terrestrial, satellite, or cable formats. With Digital Store, you can stream live TV channels and on-demand content directly to your device using our app or compatible media player. All you need is a stable internet connection.

Digital Store works with virtually all modern devices including:

  • Smart TVs (Samsung, LG, Android TV)
  • Streaming devices (Fire TV Stick, Android Box, Apple TV, Roku)
  • Mobile devices (Android phones/tablets, iPhone, iPad)
  • Computers (Windows, Mac, Linux via web browser)
  • IPTV boxes (MAG, Formuler, BuzzTV)

We recommend a minimum of 10 Mbps for SD quality, 25 Mbps for HD, and 50+ Mbps for 4K streaming. For optimal performance, use a wired Ethernet connection when possible, especially for 4K content. Multiple simultaneous streams will require higher bandwidth.

Our standard subscription allows for 2 simultaneous connections. We offer upgraded plans with more connections if needed. You can use the same account on unlimited devices, but only the allowed number can stream at the same time.

While we don't offer free trials due to abuse, we provide a 7-day money-back guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with our service within the first week, we'll refund your payment in full, no questions asked.

Setup is quick and easy:

  1. Purchase your subscription
  2. Receive login details via email
  3. Download our app or use a compatible player
  4. Enter your credentials
  5. Start streaming!
