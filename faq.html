What internet speed do I need?

We recommend the following minimum internet speeds for optimal streaming:

  • SD Quality: 10 Mbps
  • HD Quality: 25 Mbps
  • 4K Quality: 50+ Mbps

For best performance:

  • Use a wired Ethernet connection when possible
  • Close other bandwidth-intensive applications
  • Restart your router periodically
  • If using WiFi, position your device close to the router

Technical Support

Why is my stream buffering?

Buffering can occur due to several reasons. Try these troubleshooting steps:

  1. Check your internet speed: Run a speed test (fast.com or speedtest.net) to ensure you meet minimum requirements
  2. Restart your device and router: Power cycle your equipment
  3. Reduce WiFi interference: Move closer to your router or use Ethernet
  4. Close other applications: Bandwidth-heavy apps can affect streaming
  5. Try a different server: Our app allows you to switch servers in settings
  6. Check for updates: Ensure your app and device software are up-to-date

If problems persist, contact our support team with details about when buffering occurs and which channels are affected.

How do I update the channel list?

Our channel list updates automatically in most cases. If you need to manually update:

  • In our app: Go to Settings > Update Channel List
  • On third-party players: Restart your player or reload the playlist
  • On MAG/STB devices: Reboot your device

We add new channels and update existing ones regularly. Major updates are announced in our newsletter and social media channels.
